Product Details

Hugo Boss Infinite is a men's fragrance that was launched in 2019. It is a refreshing and sophisticated scent that is designed for the modern man who is always on the go. The fragrance is characterized by its energizing and invigorating blend of citrus and spice notes, which create a fresh and stimulating aroma. The top notes of Hugo Boss Infinite include sparkling apple, mandarin, and cinnamon, which provide a burst of freshness and warmth. The heart notes are composed of rosemary and lavender, which add a touch of elegance and refinement to the fragrance. The base notes consist of olive wood, sandalwood, and patchouli, which give the scent a woody and musky undertone.
